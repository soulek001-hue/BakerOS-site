FAQ

Is BakerOS only for home bakers?

No. It works for home bakers, cottage bakeries, cookie decorators, cake artists, and small bakery businesses.

Does Trial Access include messaging?

No. Trial Access includes your storefront and order tools. Messaging unlocks with Growth.

Can customers order from my storefront?

Yes. Customers can browse products, upload inspiration photos, and submit order requests.

Can I use Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, Stripe, or Square?

Yes. BakerOS can display and organize supported payment options depending on your plan.

Does BakerOS replace QuickBooks?

No. BakerOS helps organize bakery business data and can support accounting exports on higher plans.
